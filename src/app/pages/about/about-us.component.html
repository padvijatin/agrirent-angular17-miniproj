About AgriRent

Agriculture is the backbone of our country, but many farmers still face practical problems when it comes to using modern equipment. Machines like tractors, harvesters, and sprayers are useful, but buying them requires a huge investment.

AgriRent was created to solve this problem in a simple way. Instead of every farmer buying expensive equipment, the platform makes it possible to rent machinery when it is actually needed.

At the same time, many equipment owners have machines that stay unused for long periods. AgriRent connects farmers and owners so both sides can benefit.

Why We Started

The idea came from observing the gap between available resources and actual usage in agriculture. Many farmers struggle due to lack of access, while equipment often stays idle. We wanted to create one platform that solves both problems together.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make agricultural equipment easily accessible without financial pressure. We want to encourage smart farming through shared resources, simple booking, and practical use of technology.

What AgriRent Offers

  • Farmers can search equipment, compare options, and book machinery for specific dates.
  • Equipment owners can list their machines and make better use of idle equipment.
  • The system includes login, role-based access, and admin controls.
  • The Contact Us form is connected with email support and admin message management.
  • Clear pricing and a simple booking flow make the platform easy to use.

About the Project

AgriRent is developed as part of the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program. It is built using Angular, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Firebase.

This project is not only for academic work. The main idea behind it is real: to bring technology closer to agriculture in a useful and meaningful way.
